About Us

City-cleaners is a non profit organization which strives to create jobs for the unemployed by running a campaign where individuals are compensated for the amount of litter that is collected from their work and home environments. We would to create different checking sites, this is where all trash will be weighed and distributed into different mean of recycling. The vision is to create a cleaner South Africa ,but however the mission will consist of communities having regular monthly clean ups so that no garbage piles up overtime.

Vision and Mission

To manage South Africa's solid waste as a resource, promoting equitable, sustainable, environmentally sound and cost effective practices through an integrated system of waste reduction, reuse, recycling, innovative technology, customer service and education.To reduce the amount of waste by promoting individual and corporate responsibility. To recover waste for its highest and best use while balancing rates and services. To transform our business from burying waste to utilizing waste as a resource. To eliminate the need for land fills.
